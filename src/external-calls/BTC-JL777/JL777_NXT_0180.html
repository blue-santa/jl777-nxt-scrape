In general if you see a zero balance or problems in basilisk mode, first thing to do:

DONT PANIC!

Just use the kpx.io explorer and you can see your coins on the blockchain.
Just because the basilisk is sleeping, doesnt mean anything happened to your coins.

The advantage of using basilisk is that it is setup instantly. However, it does rely on servers, which go through maintenance (scheduled and unscheduled) so you can expect downtime every once in a while.

DONT PANIC!

If you coins are in the blockchain, they are fine. just wait for the basilisk to wake up and you will be able to access your coins.

It is a bit like a website that you cant access, you dont assume all your documents on that website are deleted? Just wait till you can login and there is all your email. Kind of like that, but a lot more secure as only you have the passphrase to access the funds.

DONT PANIC
Just try again when it is announced to be working again
