I did NXT <-> asset. It happened in a few seconds between the nodes, they both got valid signedtx which was then broadcast to the blockchain. The moment both nodes published to the blockchain or when the next block happened. For that matter, when did the next block actually happen? there is propagation time, plus it is sort of getting more and more "real" as time passes. until it went past 720 blocks, it was probably not totally completed.

I think this is somewhat a philosophical questions. If a signed tx is broadcast and has 99.99% chance of completing, did it actually complete the moment it was broadcast? For most practical scenarios, the answer is yes, but you do have to wait to respend what was traded for and for that you need the next block.
