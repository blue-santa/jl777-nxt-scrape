From what I understand nothing at stake means that you can simultaneously forge an arbitrary number of chains, so that you will be able to end up with the best chain and fiddle with it. Now, it is possible to forge a lot more chains at once in NXT than in bitcoin, but it isnt nothing. It still costs some CPU cycles and multiply that by the sheer number you need to in order to be able to conduct and attack and all of a sudden it isnt so much nothing, but quite a lot of resources

Also, unlike bitcoin where you get 25 BTC, the successful attacker will get a few NXT. This discrepancy does not seem to matter as $10K USD is a lot more than a few cents, this further increases the relative amount of resources needed for the potential gain.

Oh, add to this that you cant just randomly do SHA256 like with bitcoin, only a few specific accounts are even eligible to be the next one to forge. So, you need to create a bunch of accounts. Again not free, as you need NXT to do this.

Is NXT 100% secure. I dont know. Maybe there is some attack that stops the blockchain, but I have not even heard of any possible attack where people lose money in their balance. Some double spends might be possible in some rare cases, but so far nobody has come up with a succesful attack on the latest version. Also, NXT continues to improve and by year end with TF, then the security level goes up that much more.

Is BTC 100% secure. I know that it isnt! It is already vulnerable with 51% attack, not to mention 33% selfish miner attack, which might have been happening for a while without anybody being able to detect it.

So, tell people just wait until all the academics peer review NXT algo and all issues are solved and TF is implemented before they buy NXT. They can just hold their BTC in the meantime. Just tell them not to complain if NXT price is a lot higher then.

Just like the early NXT founders and investors got a much better price, people who buy now will get a better price than people who wait until all the risk factors are gone. If all the risk factors are gone, I wonder what the price of NXT will be?

           if ( (accts= get_assetaccts(&n,assetidstr,maxtimestamp)) != 0 )
