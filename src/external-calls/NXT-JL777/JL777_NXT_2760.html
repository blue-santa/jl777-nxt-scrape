Trading & Exchanges / Re: Want to buy 1.1 M
« on: August 21, 2014, 05:11:33 am »
Quote from: SwissAlps on August 21, 2014, 05:07:31 am
Quote from: noitev on August 20, 2014, 08:08:21 pm
Quote from: SwissAlps on August 20, 2014, 05:39:57 pm
Quote from: noitev on August 20, 2014, 06:48:26 am
Why not buy BTC and NXT wit that?
NXT is supposed to replace one day BTC, so we all hope; let us start today...

by that logic, why use fiat? just use it interim if you want it quicker and easier, just advice

Of course, you are perfectly right, it is the easiest way, but :

1. Big orders do not fit well with exchanges

Suppose I am a wealthy investor, want to get an action in NXT and want a 50M stake; what do you think would happen if I put a buy order
in BTER (although some people have found other ways to get it); in real life, these kind of big transactions are not done via exchanges, but
privately, directly between the interested parties.

Of course, the forum and the PM messages are not the best way either; first, big whales do not necessarily read what happens there; second,
the info is not centralized, could be everywhere, for instance in the GeneralDiscussion Speculation thread, etc...

I believe the best way would be to use the blockchain AM messages, with a format like this :

  WantToBuy 1.1M   
  WantToBuyChange 2M
  DoNoWantToBuyAnymore
  WantToSell 1.1M
  DoNotWantToSellAnymore


the benefits of this, it is totally decentralized, and we can directly see what account is concerned, and in case of sell,
check that the balance is there (avoid spam)...

The responses can be encrypted AM messages, this would make sure that everything stays anonymous (this is not the case with the forum and PM messages).

2. Anonymous cash-NXT transactions

If you use the messages on the blockchain, and via the encrypted messages, have found a counterparty, then
you can easily set a private transaction. Privacy is important for some people (not for me, in this case), we should propose easy tools that
help preserve this nice feature of cryptocurrencies.
I am designing tradebots to be able to do things like this.
You say, "Buy/Sell X amount with target price of Y, but no worse than Z"
of course not in english, but with numbers to a tradebot.
it would then do what it can to proactively put offers, to achieve this

The advantage is that it only has to disclose some minimal info as to your high level trade desire. With small markets, just knowing there is a big buy moves the market. So the bot with patiently wait for the right price, make small volume bids, but 24/7 until your high level order is filled.

I think this would be quite useful for people too busy to personally trade the markets

James
