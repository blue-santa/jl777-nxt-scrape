So, I hope it will survive a NXT core rescan, but not really possible to force that without deleting blockchain...
You can try forcing a rescan this way:
Start forging, then disconnect your node from the network so that it goes on forging on a fork. Do a transaction that cannot be undone on block pop-off - such as changing the url of existing alias, or submitting and partially filling a bid/ask order. After this transaction is accepted in a block that you forged on your fork, restore your network connection. When your node sees the main chain and tries to pop-off the fork that you forged by yourself, when it tries to pop-off the block containing the non-undoable transaction it should do a rescan.
