A certain member's phone/skype/email is buzzing with requests for interviews and speaking engagements. Of course, this is a decentralized affair and my opinion matters as much as the post above and below. If someone out there thinks marketing is the answer, start marketing!
marketable services that users want is the ONLY way to get mass market acceptance
I thought this was self-evident? I make cool services to show this. I hope others are making many more cool services.
The NXT is for building mass market services. users dont care about componenents
companies build their brand and cool service, customers pay money, companies make more services
all during this NXT is becoming like the Intel CPU. The Intel retail biz is only done for the mass market awareness and is a nice boost, but the big money is from Intel being selected in the mass market computers

Similarily, the most important channel for NXT is to the service providers. Even if no end user even knows about NXT, as long as the service providers know, then this is all that matters. Of course, the mass market awareness is never hurting, since all the service providers are made of people (at least this decade) and these people are a small part of the mass market.

So, all the people who are wanting NXT to have superbowl commercials, I suggest to change the focus to finding the service provides that use NXT who will have their superbowl commercials.

This is general challenge when it comes to marketing - should you catch the end user or the producer - I think we shall try both: if you have enough buyers , sellers will come and the other way around - if you sell something interesting and well promoted, the buyers will come (although the latter seems harder).

Speaking of the marketplace - what is the state of the reputation system? Even if we put the market place into shape for the end user ( more crypto un-friendly but user-friendly) , how are you going to explain who is selling the product , what's his/her reputation and would he/she just sell and walk away without delivery.

The users will ask, why the marketplace is missing the ebay features and if they are just going to loose their money.

When I say user friendly and not crypto friendly  I mean that themarketplace should have a website that promotes sell/buy of distributed digital goods, explaining what that is and  where is the benefit of that ...something some guys calling himself Ribbitcoin did (not pumping here,not having interest ) .
how many fully functioning decentralized marketplaces do you know of?
It is easy to say, there shall be all the features of eBay in the NXT Marketplace.
A little harder to implement it.
This is why we need the service providers. The NXT core devs have a big enough challenge to make the "CPU" and their talents are wasted to be spending the time to assemble it into a fully branded computer.

Think about this. How does the NXT Marketplace compare to the decentralized marketplace that is built into bitcoin?

So, all these eBay level functions on top of the NXT Marketplace, this is the answer and for this there is the Freemarket project that I am involved in as an advisor, but it is being developed by some others. This will be a NXTventure offering in the coming months, but it is not available yet.

So, for the marketing side, I suggest to provide the feedbacks for the tech, this is very important to make sure we have the best tech. However, to prematurely try to market something as a finished computer when it is at the component level will not be good.

The promise of having all these things is of course very good and powerful, for this moment in time the NXT ecosystem is having 30 million NXT of seed capital for seeding of revenue producing asset companies. It is these sellers that we can identify and nurture.

James
