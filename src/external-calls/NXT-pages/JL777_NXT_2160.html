I would like to separate the disbursement decision from the actual sending of funds.

I have identified a set of individuals that the community will be able to trust, but they are busy and wont have the time to be following SuperNET details.

So, if there is an official disbursement committee formed by 7 trusted members to decide whether a spending request is properly submitted by the then current SuperNET spending requestor (currently me) by a majority vote of committee members, then the 4+ members would all post a token with their approval.

A special person who is not me and not on the committee, we will call the Approver, will approve (or reject) by validating the tokens and contact the trusted holders of the funds that there is an approved spending. the approval is posted and a trustee is posting that they are available and able to sendmoney. the approver approves this, the payment goes out, the approver posts the txid, the fact it is paid and probably notifies all the trustees of this.

So the trustees are just responding to a spending authorization when they see the first PM that it was made, but there isnt the second one that says it is completed.
