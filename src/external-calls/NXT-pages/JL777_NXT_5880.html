Though matching up all change outputs to the main acct will not be easy, you will end up seeing a lot of spurious accts and most people dont even know they own them. If you want to somehow correlate and aggregate that info, then it would take longer than a day, but just getting a realtime unspent balance, no problems.

James

Very nice!

As long as they are willing to input their private keys into their Nxt-Bitcoin Wallet or hook up their initial system, we can re-turn that into Bitcoin wallet addresses, the same way Bitcoin does.. so I don't see any problems there.
I think we dont need their private keys at all. Just have them make a dust transaction to a special address, we can also encode other info into the tx, though we can pretty cheaply encode bit patterns with the smallest satoshi digits. With NXT accts being 20 digits at most, if we used the smallest three digits seven times, it would cost at most:

.00000987 * 7 = .0000609 to get all the digits of a NXT address.
Of course the bitcoin guys might filter out a purely dust tx.
