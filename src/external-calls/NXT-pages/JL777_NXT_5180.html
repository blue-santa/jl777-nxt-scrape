NXTventures / DIVIDEND ANNOUNCEMENT - 1 NXTautoDAC asset for each 4 NXTventure assets owned
« on: May 20, 2014, 03:53:16 pm »
Dividend announcement

NXTventure will issue a dividend of 250000 NXTautoDAC assets to NXTventure asset holders of record May 31 at 00:00:00 GMT, at least a block that is close to that time will be used for the dividend calculation.

Since there are 1 million total NXTventure assets, each NXTventure asset will receive 0.25 NXTautoDAC assets. Now I wish that I had given more decimals to NXTautoDAC. In the future I will issue assets with some decimals. The dividend calculation will be 1 NXTautoDAC asset for every 4 NXTventure assets you hold. I have not decided what to do yet with the accts that dont have a multiple of 4 assets. After this dividend there will be a small amount of NXTautoDAC left inside NXTventure. These assets will be sold on the open market for working capital or held for long term appreciation.

I expect the actual dividends to go out in very early June

James
