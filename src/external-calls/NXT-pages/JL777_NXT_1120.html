once it starts forging, it will continue to as long as NXT java is running
If your VPS is compromised when you start forging, then they have your password
If your VPS is compromised after you start forging, at some point in time they will get your password.

So, what I suggest is to put 10 NXT (or so) in the account on your VPS. Start it forging.

This way, even if your VPS is compromised, you lose 10NXT and it will probably even keep forging, unless the hacker is just mean and stops your forging
