Instant Transactions / Re: InstantDEX, formerly the secret project

For your review:

****
InstantDEX

InstantDEX is a service built on top of Nxt’s Multigateway service and Nxt Asset Exchange and has been shown in a proof of concept to have achieved ~5 second transaction times prior to any optimization. The goal of InstantDEX is to allow for real time trading of any Nxt Asset against any other Nxt Asset, not just Multigateway assets, and also NXT itself.

After optimization, the goal is to achieve transactions in the ~3 second timeframe. The Multigateway will need to be trusted to redeem assets but almost all of InstantDEX will be decentralized. Due to the nature of the transactions, those with good internet connections will get better performance than those with bad internet connections. InstantDEX is still in development and the discussion thread can be found at here [link to this thread].

****
Source: Nxt Glossary >>>> https://wiki.nxtcrypto.org/wiki/Glossary
you need to add NXTsubatomic to InstantDEX!

InstantDEX is a realtime market where traders will be able to find other traders in a totally peer to peer decentralized manner. The NXT blockchain (at one per minute) is used to enter the InstantDEX market and from there you will be able to see the current orderbooks and find a trade to do.

Lets say you find a trade, but it is 5 seconds after the most recent block, so you will have to wait ~55 seconds to get the trade confirmed. However, it will take more than one block to get an escrow type of sequence in place, so while the trade was agreed to in realtime, settlement became pending until the deal was done.

I wasnt satisfied with this...

So, I did some research and in addition to being able to make some useful suggestions to jean-luc on enhancing the NXTcore to allow atomic transactions, I was able to figure out how to settle the trade at the 6 second mark!

That's NXTsubatomic. It is actually independent of pretty much everything, but by itself it is not so easy to setup as you need to tell it the exact trade parameters and the other party has to put in the identical (mirror image) trade parameters. Given a matched pair, NXTsubatomic will instantly setup a direct subatomic exchange of the specified crypto, directly from the crypto wallets. No middleman. No need to even make a deposit. No escrow. Just direct exchange with the other party.

It will be like in the movies where the bad guys are getting a money transfer and you see a progress bar as it completes. The actual time it will take to complete the subatomic is something I hope to find out today as I am about to finish debugging it. Have it almost working.

InstantDEX will be calling the NXTsubatomic to settle direct crypto to crypto trades. Things are still evolving so I am not sure of all the features InstantDEX will have at this point.

It is highly probable, but still not certain that B will get confirmed. It may happen that there are too many other transactions filling all blocks and B eventually expires, while A gets lucky and is included. Or, they may both get included on a fork, then the fork is dropped and all of its transactions go back to the unconfirmed pool, but at that time the deadline for B has already passed. So, A will again get included in the real chain, but B will not. So there are various scenarios like this where it will not be guaranteed to be either both A and B, or none. @james... whats the difference between cross chain transfers and multigateway.. do they not essentially do the same thing? or is subatomic just better at it?
multigateway is crypto <-> NXT AE
