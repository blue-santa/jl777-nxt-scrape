MinHeight added. Nxt has no microchannels. I'll think more about your scheme, your proposal sent me via PM before, and other escrow schemes exist in cryptocurrencies. Let's work together on integration core features & outes services to provide maximum ever possible.
Actually micropayment channel does exist for NXT, ever since the broadcast parameter was added. All a micropayment channel is is the ability to sign a tx locally and then send it via non-blockchain to the other side, who is able to verify that it is signed and that it has the proper payment

So, you start with Alice -> Bob 10 NXT, he verifies and now he can cash it in by broadcasting, but since he knows Alice will be sending another payment soon, he doesnt
Alice changes the tx to be 20 NXT and -> Bob. Now bob can get 20NXT by broadcasting.
30 NXT -> Bob, etc. etc.

That's all a payment channel is and by making the amounts small, I guess it becomes a micropayment channel. So then the only tx is at the end when both sides are done and the final tx is broadcast. It is a bit confusing at first especially with locking in a refund, now that could be an issue as with bitcoin you get a txid for a tx that you can then use as a txin, before any of it is on the blockchain. But I think the same can be done with NXT fullHash, etc.

So, please make sure the micropayment channel method is possible. Then it will plug right into my subatomic exchanging against all the bitcoin clones.
